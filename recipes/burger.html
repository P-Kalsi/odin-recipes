Chris' Burger Recipe

Description

Chris' Burger is an internet hit with beginner food enthusiats with a pallate for simple beef burgers which is quick to make within 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 ½ teaspoons salt
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon dried basil leaves
  • 4 hamburger buns, split

Steps

  1. Preheat grill to high heat
  2. Mix together ground beef, garlic, olive oil, salt, pepper, basil
  3. Divide into four balls
  4. flatten into patties
  5. Cook patties for about 3-5 minutes each side (or until your desired temperature)
  6. Internal temperature should be at least 70°C
  7. Remove from grill and place onto buns with toppings and condiments
